About DECaF

Get a Job

This event provides company representatives a unique opportunity to interact and engage with UC San Diego's talented engineering students regarding career options, internships, and permanent or summer employment opportunities. Open exclusively to UC San Diego students and alumni, DECaF is coordinated by the Triton Engineering Student Council and over 40 pre-professional engineering student organizations at the Jacobs School of Engineering. These engineering societies, representing the spectrum of disciplines at the Jacobs School, work in close collaboration to maximize the attendance of UC San Diego's 8,500 undergraduate and graduate student engineers and to ensure the satisfaction of companies involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Please Let Us Know If You Have Any Questions!

  • For Companies
  • For Students

When and where is the Career Fair hosted?

This year DECaF has been moved to January 19th 2017 and it will run from 9am-3pm. Please arrive early ~8am to set-up and check-in. DECaF is hosted in the Price Center Ballrooms East and West in the center of UCSD campus.

How do I register for the fair?

  • 1. Please follow this link: Registration
  • 2. Choose the appropriate package of your choice and click register now.
  • 3. Fill in all the forms and complete the payment either by check or paypal.
  • 4. You should receive a confirmation, if you did not receive a confirmation please email tejas@tesc.ucsd.edu to troubleshoot the problem.

How do I pay for the Career Fair?

You can either pay by check or using paypal during the registration. Before using paypal make sure that your account is confirmed or the payment will not go through. If you did not receive a payment confirmation after using paypal, please email tejas@tesc.ucsd.edu or our technical lead Shivam at shivam@tesc.ucsd.edu.

If you choose to pay by check please make out checks to UC Regents, with the Memo "DECaF", and mail checks to the following:

  • UC San Diego, Jacobs School of Engineering
  • Attn: Fiscal Department
  • 9500 Gilman Drive
  • MC 0403
  • La Jolla, CA
  • 92093

Where do I send my company materials/banners?

Please have your materials arrive at UC San Diego between January 9th - January 17th, and we will have your boxes waiting at your table when you arrive. We have two addresses, if you are shipping small packages use this address:

  • IDEA Engineering Student Center
  • University of California at San Diego
  • 9500 Gilman Drive #0429
  • La Jolla, CA 92093

If you have large packages or a combination of the two, please send them to:

  • IDEA Engineering Student Center
  • #0429
  • 7835 Trade St., Suite 100
  • San Diego, CA 92121

Both will add an additional 1-2 business days to delivery so please send them early!

How can I add information about additional representatives?

We will soon email all the companies a link to fill in the all the additional updated information.

If you need an invoice for the check payment?

Please email tejas@tesc.ucsd.edu to request one

How can I reserve private interview rooms?

We will provide private interview rooms for all the Corporate Sponsors, we will send out a form closer to the date for you to request them. In addition all companies can request private interview rooms by contacting UCSD Career Services Center and filling in the form on port triton.
