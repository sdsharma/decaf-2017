About DECaF

Get a Job

This event provides company representatives a unique opportunity to interact and engage with UC San Diego's talented engineering students regarding career options, internships, and permanent or summer employment opportunities. Open exclusively to UC San Diego students and alumni, DECaF is coordinated by the Triton Engineering Student Council and over 40 pre-professional engineering student organizations at the Jacobs School of Engineering. These engineering societies, representing the spectrum of disciplines at the Jacobs School, work in close collaboration to maximize the attendance of UC San Diego's 8,500 undergraduate and graduate student engineers and to ensure the satisfaction of companies involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Please Let Us Know If You Have Any Questions!

When and where is the Career Fair hosted?

This year DECaF has been moved to January 19th 2017 and it will run from 9am-3pm. Please arrive early ~8am to set-up and check-in. DECaF is hosted in the Price Center Ballrooms East and West in the center of UCSD campus.

How do I register for the fair?

  • 1. Please follow this link: Registration
  • 2. Choose the appropriate package of your choice and click register now.
  • 3. Fill in all the forms and complete the payment either by check or paypal.
  • 4. You should receive a confirmation, if you did not receive a confirmation please email tejas@tesc.ucsd.edu to troubleshoot the problem.

How do I pay for the Career Fair?

You can either pay by check or using paypal during the registration. Before using paypal make sure that your account is confirmed or the payment will not go through. If you did not receive a payment confirmation after using paypal, please email tejas@tesc.ucsd.edu or our technical lead Shivam at shivam@tesc.ucsd.edu.

If you choose to pay by check please make out checks to UC Regents, with the Memo "DECaF", and mail checks to the following:

  • UC San Diego, Jacobs School of Engineering
  • Attn: Fiscal Department
  • 9500 Gilman Drive
  • MC 0403
  • La Jolla, CA
  • 92093

What is Decaf, when and where?

  • Decaf, which stands for Disciplines in Engineering Career Fair, is an annual student-run career fair which will provide you with a great opportunity to meet and connect with representatives from some of the top engineering companies.
  • Time: January 19th, Thursday at 10:00am - 3:00pm (9:00am for F.L.O. Early Access - see below)
  • Location: Price Center Ballrooms (East & West)

What time should I get there and where should I go?

The lines will be long, so as soon as possible!

Where should I start lining up?

First things first, make sure to check the company map on the DECaF website to know which ballroom (East or West) you want to target first. There will be separate lines for both ballrooms.

East Ballroom Instructions:

  • 1. All lines will start on the Starbucks and Library Walk side of Price Center.
  • 2. If you are F.L.O, line up near Starbucks, up the stairs to PC East Ballroom (double doors). Your line will extend down towards Warren Mall and the Jacobs School of Engineering.
  • 3. If not, line up near the East Ballroom double door entrance from the Shogun / 2nd floor balcony near PC Theatre. Your line will extend around the upper balcony, down towards library walk.

West Ballroom Instructions:

  • 1. All lines start right outside West Ballroom double doors, near the UCSD Bookstore and Perks Coffee Shop on the side near YogurtWorld and the Triton Statue.
  • 2. If you are F.L.O, line up on the left side, your line will extend up towards library walk.
  • 3. If not, line up on the right side, your line will be shaped like an ‘L’, bending around the Triton Statue and all the way around PC near student services for rain cover.

Who can attend?

All UCSD students and alumni with valid UCSD ID.

What companies are attending this year?

Please refer to the company list above.

What is the dress code to the event?

There is no dress code for the event, but business casual is recommended.

What should I bring to DECaF?

  • 1. UCSD ID
  • 2. ~20-25 copies of your updated resume
  • 3. 30 second elevator pitch to sell yourself to a company representative
  • 4. Bright smile and positive energy!
  • 5. Umbrella or rain jacket in case of inclement weather

Is there any way that I can get early access to the event?

  • 1. Yes there is! Please attend at least one of four F.L.O. early access events organized by the engineering student organizations to get into DECaF an hour earlier than any other student. Please see our facebook page for an updated list of F.L.O. events.
  • 2. The first 100 students to attend each of the events will be issued a nontransferable eventbrite ticket, which will need to be presented to get early access.
  • 3. Only F.L.O. students are allowed entry into the fair between 9 and 10, after which all students will be allowed on a first come first serve basis, dependent on room.
